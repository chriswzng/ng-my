Code of Conduct

ng-MY is for the people. That's it. We want every single person to feel safe and welcome. We want everyone to enjoy this conference to the fullest. To achieve that, we have defined a Code of Conduct, an agreement in which you will take an active role whether you know it or not. This agreement is simple: Be kind to everyone at all times regarless of gender, gender identity, age, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, race, ethnicity, nationality, religion, political views or experience.

Enforcement

Breaking this agreement does not take any effort, it takes ignorance, the ignorance of our greatest gift: Diversity.

Avoid any type of harassment, sexual language or imaginery, flirting, political topics, offensive/mean jokes or threats. We take violations very seriously and we reserve the right of expelling you from the conference and our community.

Reporting Violations

If at some point you feel that this agreement has been violated, if you feel like someone is harassing you, please don't think twice, speak up, not just for you, but for others who do not have the courage to do so.
